History

TechCon began in 2018 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in Nairobi, Kenya. Over the years, it has grown into a globally recognized conference, attracting thousands of attendees with its innovative sessions and cutting-edge discussions. The event has evolved from a local meetup to a premier platform for technology and innovation.

Mission

Our mission at TechCon 2024 is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education in the tech industry. We aim to inspire the next generation of developers and provide a space for networking and knowledge sharing. Our driving principles include inclusivity, sustainability, and cutting-edge technology adoption.
